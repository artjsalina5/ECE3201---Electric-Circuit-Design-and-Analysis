Spice Netlists for Microelectronic Circuits
Nijwm Wary
Tony Chan Carusone
November 2019

Netlists are provided in text format with a file extension .cir.
These files may be opened and simulated, as is, in
- LTSpice XVII
- PSpice Version 9.1

More detailed instructions for opening and simulating these netlists in LTSpice and PSpice are available at the website for the textbook:

Microelectronic Circuits
A. Sedra, K.C. Smith, T. Chan Carusone, V. Gaudet
